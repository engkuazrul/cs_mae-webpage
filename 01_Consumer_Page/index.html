Welcome to a world of faster, easier and safer payments and transfers with Scan & Pay.

Why choose Scan & Pay — all day, every day?

Scan & Pay is a cashless feature that allows you to make payments or receive money via any accepted QR codes, including DuitNow QR. You can Scan & Pay in-store, online and even among your friends!

  • Say goodbye to the hassle of handling physical cash and loose change.

  • Accepted at over 450,000 merchants nationwide, both in-store and online.

  • Enjoy total convenience by scanning any branded DuitNow QR codes to pay.

  • Easily transfer and receive money between your friends and family members.

  • Send money anytime, anywhere by uploading your friend’s QR code from your gallery.

  • Take part in exclusive cashback promotions throughout the year.
